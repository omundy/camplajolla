Camp Matthews Sentry Booth

Located at the former rear entrance to Camp Matthews, this sentry booth remains in place on the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) campus.

The drawings and graffiti on the inside of this structure mark the moments when young Marines from all over the United States stood watch here. Now in its memorial state, plexiglas has been added to protect the markings.
