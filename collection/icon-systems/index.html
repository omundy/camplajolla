Icon Systems

Icon Systems is a San Diego defense contractor recently acquired by SAIC. They manufacture a broad range of war making machines including combat vehicle systems, wireless targeting systems, and individual weapon systems.

Icon Systems recruits at UCSD Job & Internship Fairs.
