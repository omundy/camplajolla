Seaweed Canyon Quonset Storage (former Camp Matthews building)

Seaweed Canyon Quonset Storage was completed in 1942 and occupied in 1974.

Storage space used by four departments: The Geosciences Research Division, Integrative Oceanography Division, Marine Biology Research Division, and SIO Director's Office.
