Devil Dogs of the Air (1935)

In Devil Dogs of the Air James Cagney stars as a barnstorming pilot who joins the Navy. Cagney and Pat O'Brien get tangled up with each other, a waitress at a diner, and a mock invasion of Black's Beach in La Jolla.
