William J. von Liebig Center for Entrepreneurism and Technology Advancement

The William J. von Liebig Center for Entrepreneurism and Technology Advancement works with CONNECT and the Center for Commercialization of Advanced Technology (CCAT) to promote "the commercialization of new technologies and facilitate the start-up of new business ventures" and to advance Jacobs School discoveries into the marketplace. They are part of a conduit that stretches between the academic institution and organizations directly linked to the creation of killing machines.
